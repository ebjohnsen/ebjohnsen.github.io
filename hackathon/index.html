DART Hackathon at Nesoddtangen

Hackathon : Nesoddtangen, 10-12 June 2025

DART organised a hackathon on building a digital twin of the Oslo Fjord, hosted by Marinreparatørene at Nesoddtangen. Our site for the hackathon was an underwater communal garden operated by Marinreparatørene. In the hackathon, we explored various devices for collecting live data, including both self-made and off-the-shelf sensors and video streaming.
